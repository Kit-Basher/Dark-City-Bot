# Dark City RPG Character Aspects

## Street / Survival (20)
1. **Streetwise** - *Positive:* Know the unwritten rules, navigate urban dangers. *Negative:* Might not understand "proper" society, suspicious of authority.
2. **Scrappy** - *Positive:* Fight dirty and survive against odds. *Negative:* Lack finesse, resort to violence too quickly.
3. **Hardened** - *Positive:* Tough, don't break easily. *Negative:* Difficulty connecting emotionally, callous.
4. **Desperate** - *Positive:* Push beyond normal limits when needed. *Negative:* Make poor decisions, easily exploited.
5. **Paranoid** - *Positive:* Notice threats others miss, hard to ambush. *Negative:* Struggle to trust, see enemies everywhere.
6. **Resourceful** - *Positive:* Make do with what you have, improvise solutions. *Negative:* Hoard things, refuse proper tools.
7. **Wary** - *Positive:* Careful, don't walk into traps. *Negative:* Miss opportunities, slow to act.
8. **Adaptable** - *Positive:* Adjust to new situations quickly. *Negative:* Lack core principles, go with the flow too much.
9. **Jaded** - *Positive:* Not shocked by horrors, keep composure. *Negative:* Apathetic, miss beauty or hope.
10. **Opportunistic** - *Positive:* Seize chances when they arise. *Negative:* Betray allies for personal gain.
11. **Reckless** - *Positive:* Brave, take bold action. *Negative:* Endanger yourself and others needlessly.
12. **Gritty** - *Positive:* Persevere through pain and hardship. *Negative:* Refuse help, ignore own limits.
13. **Scarred** - *Positive:* Survived serious threats, intimidating. *Negative:* Carry physical reminders of trauma.
14. **Cautious** - *Positive:* Think before acting, minimize risk. *Negative:* Hesitate at critical moments.
15. **Persistent** - *Positive:* Never give up, outlast opposition. *Negative:* Can't recognize when to quit.
16. **Overextended** - *Positive:* Juggle multiple obligations, connected. *Negative:* Spread too thin, unreliable.
17. **Battle-worn** - *Positive:* Combat experience, hard to rattle. *Negative:* Old injuries flare up, PTSD triggers.
18. **Hungry** - *Positive:* Motivated, want more from life. *Negative:* Never satisfied, take unnecessary risks.
19. **Cornered** - *Positive:* Fight hardest when trapped. *Negative:* Feel trapped even when not, lash out.
20. **Ruthless** - *Positive:* Do what's necessary to win. *Negative:* Burn bridges, create enemies.
